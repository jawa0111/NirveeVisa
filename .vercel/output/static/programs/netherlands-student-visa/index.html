Study Permit

Netherlands Student Visa

Experience innovative education in the Netherlands with a Provisional Residence Permit (MVV) and Residence Permit.

school

Eligibility Requirements

  • Admission letter from a recognized Dutch educational institution
  • Completed MVV application (for stays over 90 days)
  • Proof of sufficient financial means
  • Valid health insurance
  • TB test certificate (for certain nationalities)
  • English or Dutch language proficiency
attach_money

Financial Requirements

You must demonstrate sufficient funds:

  • Approximately €12,000 per year for living expenses
  • Proof of paid tuition fees or scholarship
  • Bank guarantee or sponsor letter accepted
work

Work Rights

Non-EU students can work up to 16 hours per week during the academic year or full-time during June, July, and August. Work permits are required but often arranged by employers.

update

Orientation Year Permit

Graduates can apply for an Orientation Year (zoekjaar) permit, allowing 1 year to find skilled employment. After finding suitable work, you can transition to a Highly Skilled Migrant visa with a reduced salary requirement.
