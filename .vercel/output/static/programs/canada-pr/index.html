PR Pathway

Canada PR (Express Entry)

Fast-track your permanent residence in Canada through the Express Entry system.

stars

Express Entry Programs

  • Federal Skilled Worker (FSW) - For skilled professionals with foreign work experience
  • Canadian Experience Class (CEC) - For those with Canadian work experience
  • Federal Skilled Trades (FST) - For qualified tradespeople
  • All programs use the Comprehensive Ranking System (CRS)
trending_up

CRS Points System

Maximum 1,200 points based on:

  • Age (max 110 points for ages 20-29)
  • Education (max 150 points for PhD)
  • Language proficiency (max 136 points per language)
  • Work experience (max 80 points Canadian, 50 foreign)
  • Provincial nomination (600 bonus points)
  • Arranged employment (up to 200 points)
attach_money

Financial Requirements

Settlement funds required (2024 estimates):

  • Single applicant: CAD $13,757
  • Family of 2: CAD $17,127
  • Family of 3: CAD $21,055
  • Family of 4: CAD $25,564
  • Proof of funds letter from bank required
schedule

Application Process & Timeline

Steps to PR:

  • Step 1: Create Express Entry profile (valid 12 months)
  • Step 2: Get ITA in bi-weekly draws (current cutoff ~500+ CRS)
  • Step 3: Submit complete application within 60 days
  • Processing time: 6 months from complete application
  • Permanent Resident Card valid for 5 years
emoji_events

Path to Citizenship

After PR:

  • Live in Canada for 3 out of 5 years (1,095 days)
  • File taxes for at least 3 years
  • Pass citizenship test (knowledge of Canada)
  • Language proficiency proof required (CLB 4)
  • Apply for Canadian citizenship
