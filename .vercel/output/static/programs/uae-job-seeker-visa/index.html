Explore career opportunities in the UAE with flexible job seeker and green visa options.

UAE Green Visa (Skilled Workers)

  • Bachelor's degree or equivalent qualification
  • Minimum 3 years of work experience
  • Salary not less than AED 15,000 per month
  • Valid employment contract in UAE
  • No sponsor or employer required for renewal
attach_money

Job Seeker Visa Options

Multiple entry visa for job hunting:

  • 60-day, 90-day, and 180-day options available
  • Does not require a sponsor or host
  • Valid for single or multiple entries
  • Apply through ICP (Federal Authority)
  • Fee varies by duration (approx. AED 1,500-3,000)
schedule

Green Visa Benefits

Advantages of the Green Visa category:

  • 5-year renewable residency
  • No employer sponsorship needed after initial
  • Sponsor parents and children up to 25 years
  • Unlimited support staff (domestic workers)
  • Grace period: 6 months after cancellation/expiration
groups

Application Process

Steps to apply:

  • Apply online through ICP Smart Services
  • Submit documents (degree, passport, photo)
  • Pay visa fees online
  • Medical fitness test in UAE (after arrival)
  • Emirates ID registration
